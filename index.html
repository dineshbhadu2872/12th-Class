Chapter 1: Communication Skills

1. What is Communication?

The process of exchanging information, ideas, or feelings between individuals using verbal or non-verbal methods.

2. Types of Communication

  • Verbal: Spoken or written words (e.g., speeches, emails).
  • Non-Verbal: Body language, gestures, facial expressions.
  • Visual: Charts, graphs, images.

3. Barriers to Effective Communication

  1. Language differences
  2. Noise or distractions
  3. Lack of attention
  4. Cultural differences

4. 7 C's of Communication

For effective communication, ensure your message is:

  • Clear
  • Concise
  • Concrete
  • Correct
  • Coherent
  • Complete
  • Courteous
